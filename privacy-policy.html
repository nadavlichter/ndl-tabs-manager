Privacy Policy

Introduction

At TabToolKit, we take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles data and ensures your privacy is protected.

We believe in complete transparency about our practices, so you can make informed decisions about using our extension.

Data Collection Policy

TabToolKit does not collect ANY user information or browsing data. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history
  • Tab contents
  • URLs visited
  • Search queries
  • Login credentials
  • Form data

All tab management functionality operates locally on your device. Your data never leaves your browser.

Local Storage

TabToolKit stores your preferences and settings (such as custom tab groups and themes) locally in your browser's storage. This information is:

  • Never transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Only accessible by the TabToolKit extension
  • Automatically removed if you uninstall the extension

Browser Permissions

TabToolKit requires certain browser permissions to function properly. These permissions are used exclusively for tab management features and not for data collection:

  • Tabs permission - to organize and manage your tabs
  • Storage permission - to save your preferences locally

We request only the minimum permissions necessary for our extension to function.

Safe for Business Use

Because TabToolKit does not collect, transmit, or store any user data or browsing information, it is completely safe for both personal and business use. Our extension complies with:

  • Corporate data security policies
  • Industry privacy standards
  • Confidentiality requirements for sensitive work environments

Third-Party Access

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to share with third parties. Your browsing activities remain completely private.

Updates to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the version in the Chrome Web Store and provide a notification within the extension.

For significant changes, we may also notify users via the email associated with their Chrome Web Store account (if available).
